What We Do

We help navigate your brand through the ever-changing digital world to inspire action between companies and consumers.

RABBIT STUDIO was founded in the heart of San Francisco in 2013 by partners Adam Smith and Eric Johnson. Both have worked in some of California's top creative agencies where their expertise was highly valued. After a few years of freelancing as a team they decided that they wanted to start a new type of agency - a fresh approach that combines genius design with beautiful interactive user experiences.

Our strategy is simple- we aim to understand your concepts and goals to inspire fresh innovative ideas that will help you stand out & more importantly succeed in this competitive market. We understand that each company is unique and that not all solutions are the same. That is why we provide customized approaches to suit your needs.
